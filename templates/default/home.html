HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Your health, our mission

HMS

The HMS system targets  to  provide complete  solution  for all needed Hospital related services. The intended audience
includes all stakeholders in the potential system which include the Administrative Staff, patients and doctors. 


The fastest medical care now at your doorstep

Hospital Management System which provides online real time information
about the doctors available, number of beds available, suggestion of doctors,
etc. This is a software from where a patient can get the entire details of the
hospital as well as all the infrastructure those are available in the hospital.


Features

The HMS system will provide different types of services
based on the user. The admin of the hospital will be working like the controller and can administer the database of various users. While the patients and the doctors  have got other functionalities.


Patient's profile

A one stop destination made exclusively for our patients to access all medical facilities starting from booking doctor's appointments to payment of bills all from the comfort of their homes.

Doctor's profile

An easy access to a well managed repository that comprises of all the patient details, diagnosis information, current status etc.

Admin portal

A well developed network to regulate all our administrative work.

Easy to use Interface

A simplified interface supported with a well developed database to store information.
